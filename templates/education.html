Digital Safety Education

Empowering students with the knowledge to navigate the digital world safely and responsibly.

Why Scan?

Safety systems act as a protective layer, helping keep our school community focused on learning by identifying potentially harmful content before it spreads.

Human Review

AI makes mistakes. That's why every flag is reviewed by a real person who understands context, like art projects or beach photos.

Your Privacy

We respect your digital boundaries. Images are scanned in real-time and never stored on our servers. Your identity remains private.

Do
  • Think before you share
  • Respect others' privacy and consent
  • Use school accounts for educational purposes
  • Report concerning content to a trusted adult
Don't
  • Share images without consent
  • Post or share inappropriate content
  • Be afraid to ask for help if content is flagged
  • Assume the AI is always correct
