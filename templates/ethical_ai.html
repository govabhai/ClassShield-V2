Ethical AI Principles

ClassShield is built upon a foundation of responsibility, ensuring that school safety technology respects student dignity and rights.

Human Sovereignty

Algorithms suggest, humans decide. No student content is ever deleted automatically, ensuring context is always considered by a trained administrator.

Total Transparency

We provide full explainability for every flag, showing exact model scores and detection methods to build trust with students and parents.

Privacy by Design

Student privacy is paramount. Images are processed in memory and not permanently stored unless there's an active incident requiring investigation.

Bias Awareness

Comprehensive testing across demographics (Fitzpatrick Types I-VI, multiple age groups). We monitor for disparate impact and document limitations. No claims of perfect fairness - human review required for all edge cases.

These principles are integrated into every line of code in ClassShield, from the multi-tier detection logic to the admin review workflow.
